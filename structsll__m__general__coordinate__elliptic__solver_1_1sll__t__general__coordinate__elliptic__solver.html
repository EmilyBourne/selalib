Detailed Description

General coordinate elliptic solver derived type.

The indexing of the splines in array global_indices depends on the boundary conditions. local_indices includes the changes resulting from the boundary conditions. local_to_lobal_indices(i,j) = global_indices(local_indices(i,j))
